# Dashboard

A comprehensive career analytics dashboard that transforms your JSON Resume into visual metrics, charts, and insights.

## How to Access

- **URL**: `registry.jsonresume.org/{username}/dashboard`
- **Auth**: GitHub OAuth login required

## Features

### Career Metrics

The dashboard computes and displays key metrics from your resume data:

- **Total years of experience** calculated from work history
- **Number of positions** held across your career
- **Skills count** and categorization
- **Education history** summary

### Skills Analysis

Visual breakdown of your skills section:

- Skill categories and keyword counts
- Relative proficiency indicators (when level data is available)
- Technology stack overview

### Career Progression

Charts showing your career trajectory over time:

- Job title progression
- Company changes
- Duration at each position
- Gaps and overlaps in employment

### Education Summary

Overview of your educational background including:

- Institutions attended
- Degrees and areas of study
- Relevant coursework

## Usage Guide

1. Log in at `registry.jsonresume.org` with GitHub
2. Navigate to `registry.jsonresume.org/{username}/dashboard`
3. Review your career metrics at a glance
4. Explore the skills analysis for insights on your strengths
5. Check the career progression charts for patterns

## Tips & Best Practices

- A more complete resume produces richer analytics — fill in all dates and details
- Use the dashboard to identify gaps before applying to jobs
- Combine insights with [AI Suggestions](/ai-features/suggestions) for targeted improvements
- Check the dashboard after updating your resume to see the changes reflected
