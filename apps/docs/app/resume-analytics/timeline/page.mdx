# Timeline

A visual career timeline that displays your work history and education in a chronological, alternating layout.

## How to Access

- **URL**: `registry.jsonresume.org/{username}/timeline`
- **Auth**: Public (uses your public resume data)

## Features

### Visual Timeline

Your career history is displayed as an alternating left-right timeline:

- **Work experience** entries with company, title, dates, and description
- **Education** entries with institution, degree, and dates
- **Date markers** showing the chronological progression
- **Alternating layout** with entries on left and right for visual clarity

### Public Access

The timeline is publicly accessible for any username. A banner at the top indicates when viewing in public mode, with a prompt to log in for the full experience.

### Responsive Design

The timeline adapts to different screen sizes:

- Desktop: Full alternating layout
- Tablet: Compact alternating layout
- Mobile: Single-column stacked layout

## Usage Guide

1. Navigate to `registry.jsonresume.org/{username}/timeline`
2. Scroll through your career history chronologically
3. Share the URL with others — it's publicly accessible

## Tips & Best Practices

- Include start and end dates on all work and education entries for an accurate timeline
- The timeline is a great way to share your career story with recruiters
- Use it to visualize gaps or overlaps in your employment history
- Combine with the [Dashboard](/resume-analytics/dashboard) for a complete career overview
